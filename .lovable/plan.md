
# IMCJL – Interim Management & Consulting Website

A single-page, board-level professional website with calm authority, clean architecture, and controlled motion.

## Design System
- **Colors**: White dominant background, deep navy (#1B2A4A) for dark sections, light blue accents
- **Typography**: Inter font family — clean, structured, modern
- **Style**: Thin structural lines, no gradients, no stock photos, abstract architectural line grid elements
- **Motion**: Subtle fade-in animations on scroll, controlled line animations in hero

---

## Sections (Top to Bottom)

- Left-aligned headline: *"Transforming Operations. Stabilizing Performance. Delivering Results."*
- Subheadline about interim management for logistics & automotive
- Two CTA buttons: "Discuss Your Situation" (primary) and "View Case Experience" (secondary, scrolls down)

### 8. Minimal Footer

## Navigation
- Fixed top navbar with smooth scroll to sections
- Minimal: Logo/name + section links + primary CTA button

- Sections stack cleanly on smaller screens
